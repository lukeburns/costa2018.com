Listening First

We'd like for you to have routine and tangible influence over our campaign and, if elected, office. We will begin every two weeks with listening points, and what we hear will guide our decision making.

We will bring these listening points with us, door to door and digitally, to connect with as diverse a population as we can. After each cycle, we will publish the stories we have heard and the influences they had on our campaign.

Provide an email if you'd like a response from us. We'll never share your email with anyone else.

About Costa

Recent adventures include working with NASA scientists to rethink the search for life beyond Earth, designing an innovation tournament inside of a Fortune 500 to seed innovation projects, speaking at the United Nations in New York as part of the first TEDx event there, working with a group of cyber security experts (including one of the original creators of the internet—not Al Gore) to make recommendations to the Obama Administration, and that’s just the compulsory who’s who. Donate

The FEC has some rules you should know about before donating:

  1. You must be a U.S. citizen or lawfully admitted permanent resident (i.e., green card holder).
  2. This contribution must be made from your own funds, and funds must not be provided to you by another person or entity for the purpose of making this contribution.
  3. You must be making this contribution with your own personal credit card and not with a corporate or business credit card or a card issued to another person.
  4. You must be at least eighteen years old.
  5. You must not be a federal contractor.
